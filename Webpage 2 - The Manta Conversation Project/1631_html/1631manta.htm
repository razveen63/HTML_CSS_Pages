Background

Distribution

Mantas are found worldwide, in tropical and temperate waters. This area includes South Africa, Madagascar, Somalia to Mozambique, Red Sea, Arabian Sea, bay of Bengal, Myanmar(Burma), Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, Philippines, Cambodia, Japan, northern Australia, Micronesia, New Caledonia, Fiji, New Guinea, Solomon Islands, Hawaii, southern California to northern Peru, North Carolina to Brazil, Senegal to Liberia and the Azores.
